Guide to Moon Phases and Tides

  • New Moon: Spring tides (high highs and low lows).
  • Full Moon: Similar to New Moon.
  • First Quarter: Neap tides (moderate highs and lows).
  • Last Quarter: Similar to First Quarter.

Tip: Spring tides are ideal for strong waves, while neap tides offer stability.

Influences on Wave Formation

Wind

Wind generates waves by transferring energy to the ocean surface:

  • Wind Speed: Faster winds create larger waves.
  • Duration: Longer wind duration leads to bigger waves.
  • Fetch: Distance over which wind blows, allowing waves to grow.

Ocean Depth

Depth affects wave behavior near the shore:

  • Deep Water Waves: Maintain energy and speed.
  • Shallow Water Waves: Slow down and break near the shore.

Geological Features

  • Seafloor Topography: Reefs and sandbars influence wave breaking.
  • Coastal Shape: Headlands and bays affect wave strength.
