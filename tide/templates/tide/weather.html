Guide to Moon Phases and Tides

  • New Moon: Spring tides (high highs and low lows). Great for strong waves.
  • Full Moon: Spring tides (similar to New Moon).
  • First Quarter: Neap tides (moderate highs and lows). Best for beginners.
  • Last Quarter: Neap tides (similar to First Quarter).

Tip: Spring tides provide stronger waves, ideal for experienced surfers. Neap tides are more stable, better for beginners.
